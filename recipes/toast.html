Toast

How To Make Toast

Description

Toast is really delicious, and good for the soul. If you want that goodness, here's how to make some.

Ingredients

  • bread
  • butter
  • salt(optional)
  • honey(optional)

Steps

  1. Use a knife to extract butter
  2. Spread button on bread
  3. Add salt if in the mood for something savory
  4. Add honey if in the mood for something sweet
