How To Make Cereal

Description

Cereal is really delicious, and good for the soul. If you want that goodness, here's how to make a bowl.

Ingredients

  • cereal
  • milk
  • banana
  • dried cranberries

Steps

  1. Pour the cereal into a bowl
  2. Pour the milk over the cereal
  3. Add bananas and dried berries
  4. Use spoon to eat
