How To Make Beef Tacos

Description

Tacos are crunchy, messy and great fun to eat, so it’s no wonder they’re a big hit with the kids.

They’re also easy to make – more of an assembly job than a recipe.

Ingredients

  • tortilla
  • black beans
  • tomatos
  • cheese
  • beef

Steps

  1. Heat up the tortilla
  2. Heat up the black beans
  3. Cook beef
  4. Add cheese and tomatos
